1. Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how [shs-uplord short] ("the Service") collects,
uses, and protects your information when you use the Service to upload
short-form videos to your own social media accounts.
2. Information We Collect
The Service collects only the minimum information needed to function:
- Authentication tokens (OAuth access and refresh tokens) provided by
platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and others when
you authorize the Service.
- Basic account identifiers (such as your account ID or username) returned
by those platforms during authentication.
- Video files and related metadata (title, caption, tags) that you choose
to upload.

The Service does NOT collect your password. Authentication is handled
entirely through the official OAuth process of each platform.

1. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information solely to:
- Authenticate your accounts through official OAuth.
- Upload videos to the accounts you have explicitly authorized.
- Display the status of your uploads.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose
unrelated to publishing your own content.

1. Data Sharing
We do NOT sell, rent, share, or transfer your personal information or
access tokens to any third party. Your data is used only to operate the
Service on your behalf.

Information is transmitted only to the official platform APIs (such as
the TikTok Content Posting API) for the purpose of uploading your content.

1. Data Storage and Security
- Access tokens are stored securely and used only to perform uploads you
have requested.
- We take reasonable measures to protect your information from
unauthorized access.
1. Data Retention and Deletion
- We retain your access tokens only for as long as you keep your account
connected to the Service.
- You may revoke the Service's access at any time through your platform
settings (for example, TikTok app settings). Once access is revoked or
you request deletion, all stored tokens and related data are deleted.
- To request deletion of your data, contact us at: [shs.marketing.001@gmail.com]
1. Data from TikTok
Any data obtained through TikTok is used only to upload videos to your own
authorized TikTok account. We do not use TikTok data for any other purpose
and do not share it with third parties.
2. Children's Privacy
The Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18 (or the
minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect data
from minors.
3. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version
